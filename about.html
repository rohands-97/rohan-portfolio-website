I’m Rohan, a product designer with 2+ years of experience in graphic design. I blend my creative expertise with strategic thinking to craft intuitive, meaningful, and accessible digital experiences. I’m passionate about my work because I’ve always wanted to do something that helps people and makes their lives easier.

When I’m not designing, you’ll either find me with my pencil and sketchbook, at the gym, or in the kitchen experimenting with new dishes. I also love travelling, and I’m a big believer that nature can offer real-life ideas and experiences that you can bring into design.
